Career Opportunities & Scope

Completing LinkIt's Air Ticketing & Reservations course equips learners with straddling soft and hard skills, preparing them for various occupations, including:

  • Travel Consultant / Ticketing Agent: Employed by retail travel agencies and corporate travel management companies, as well as in blended online-offline travel studios.
  • Airline Reservations Executive: Employed by call centres and reservation offices of airlines, handling large volumes of requests from passengers and achieving optimal load factors.
  • Corporate Travel Specialist: Serving large corporations with their travel requirements, negotiating discounted fares and administrating the profiles of corporate travellers.
  • E-Commerce & Online Travel Agency (OTA) Coordinator: Responsibilities include overseeing inventory uploads, managing fare restrictions for dynamic packaging, and coordinating with hotel, car-rental, and other ancillary service vendors.
  • Revenue Management Assistant: Assisting airline revenue management teams by interpreting bookings, adjusting fare classes, and making demand forecasts.

In 2025, with global travel set to resume in full swing due to released demand, the normalisation of business travel, and new markets opening up, skilled ticketing professionals will be in high demand. From small travel startup ventures to global travel companies, in many cases, career paths accelerate within 2-3 years after certification, mainly due to the travel industry's structure in supervising, managerial, and strategic roles.
