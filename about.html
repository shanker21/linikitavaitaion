About Us

Let your ambitions take flight - with Linkit Aviation.

LinkIt Aviation Academy is the newest destination for aviation and hospitality training built for the next generation of professionals. Created for students aged 18 and above, we combine the latest learning modules, modern classrooms, advanced labs, and strong industry connections to make sure you gain real-world skills from day one. Located in Begumpet, Hyderabad, we bring fresh energy, modern facilities, and career-focused training to help you stand out in a competitive job market.

Our Mission

We provide top-notch, practical training in aviation services and hospitality. Our focus covers everything from ground handling and gate operations to customer service and airport terminal management. We aim to transform enthusiastic learners into capable professionals who can help these fast-growing fields continue to expand.

Our Vision

To be recognised as one of India’s most trusted and innovative training academies for aviation and hospitality services, known for producing job-ready professionals.

Core Principles

Student Growth First

Every decision we make from course design to inviting guest trainers is focused on helping our students grow, learn, and succeed.

Hands-On Experience

From the very first day, our students work in simulation labs, practise real tasks, and build practical skills that employers value.

Integrity & Transparency

We believe in being honest and transparent with students, parents, and industry partners through open and regular communication.

What Makes Us Different

Fresh Perspective

As a new academy, we’re not tied to old ways. We adopt the latest training methods, tools, and technology faster.

Lean & Responsive

Our size helps us adapt quickly to student feedback and industry needs.

Personalised Attention

Smaller class sizes mean one-on-one guidance, mentoring, and immediate support from instructors.

Built for the Future

Our facilities, courses, and partnerships are designed to meet 2025 industry standards without outdated systems holding us back.

Campus & Facilities

Modern Classrooms

Interactive spaces with the latest AV technology for practical and engaging lessons.
